Luma Launcher Privacy Policy

Effective July 16, 2026

Luma Launcher is an Android TV home screen. It has no advertising, analytics, or user accounts. Luma does not sell personal information.

Weather and approximate location

When Automatic weather is selected, Luma contacts ipwho.is. That service receives the TV's internet address and returns an approximate city and coordinates. Luma then sends those approximate coordinates to Open-Meteo to retrieve weather. Luma does not store the internet address or location on a server. You can instead select a preset city.

Accessibility Home helper

The optional Home helper uses Android Accessibility only to receive a window-change event containing the package name of a known stock Android TV Home screen. When that screen appears, Luma reopens itself. The helper cannot retrieve window content and does not read typed text, passwords, or personal information. Accessibility data is not stored, sent off the device, sold, or shared.

Music widget

If you grant Notification access, Luma reads the active Android media session on the device to show the current title, artist, app name, artwork, and playback state and to operate play or pause. This information is processed only on the TV and is not transmitted.

Apps, network, and personal choices

  • Installed launchable app names and icons are read to create the launcher and remain on the device.
  • Connection type, local network address, and VPN status are read and displayed only on the device.
  • Favorites, appearance settings, and user-selected backgrounds or custom icons remain on the device.

Updates and retention

The Google Play version is updated through Google Play. Luma has no developer-operated server and retains no user data off-device. Removing the app or clearing its data removes its locally saved preferences.

Questions

Privacy questions can be submitted through the Luma Launcher issue tracker.
